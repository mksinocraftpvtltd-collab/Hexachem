About HEXACHEM

Six Strong, by name and by nature.

We're a specialty chemical trading and distribution company built on a simple idea: the chemistry behind great work should be reliable, verifiable, and traceable — every single time.

Who we are

HEXACHEM sources and distributes specialty chemicals for construction, coatings, waterproofing and industrial performance. We sit between the world's manufacturers and the people who build with their chemistry — and we take responsibility for everything in between.

That means vetting suppliers so you don't have to, verifying every lot against spec, handling and repackaging to your requirements, and delivering on schedule with the documentation to prove what's inside. The unglamorous work — done exceptionally well.

The name says it: Six Strong. Our Constellation mark is six points held in perfect balance — a reminder that dependable supply is the sum of many things done right, consistently, with nothing left to chance.
