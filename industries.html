Who we serve, and what we're accountable for

Our product catalog is the chemistry we trade. This page is the other half of the story — the people and projects that chemistry goes into, and the outcome we take responsibility for in each.

Builders & Contractors

Concrete waits for no one. We keep admixtures, curing compounds and grouts arriving on time and on spec, so your pours hit strength and your programme doesn't slip.

Typical supplySuperplasticizers · set control · grouts · curing compounds

Coatings & Ink Producers

A formulation is only as repeatable as its inputs. We supply resins, pigments and additives with the lot-to-lot consistency that colour-matching and durability depend on.

Typical supplyResins · pigments · driers · rheology additives

Waterproofing Specialists

Failure here is invisible until it's expensive. We source crystalline, bituminous and acrylic systems that perform under hydrostatic pressure for the long service life you promise.

Typical supplyRepair mortars · protective coatings · sealants · additives
