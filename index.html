What we do

More than a link in your supply chain

From origin sourcing and verification to repackaging and traceable delivery, we manage the parts of specialty supply that slow projects down.

SOURCING

Global sourcing network

Direct relationships with vetted manufacturers across four continents — dependable availability and competitive pricing, even on hard-to-find grades.
VERIFICATION

Tested & documented

Every lot verified against spec with a documented certificate of analysis — what arrives is exactly what your process was validated for.
HANDLING

Blending & repackaging

Custom packaging from drums to IBCs and bulk, plus toll blending to your formulation — handled in compliant, climate-controlled facilities.
LOGISTICS

Traceable logistics

Hazmat-certified freight, real-time tracking and inventory programs that keep your line running without you carrying the storage risk.
